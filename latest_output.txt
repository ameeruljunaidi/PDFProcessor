- Ivanhoe Electric closed acquisition of private land at Santa Cruz Copper Project in Arizona, giving control over both surface land and mineral rights.
- Final purchase price reduced by $3.63 million due to adjustment in acreage.
- Ivanhoe Electric paid $34.3 million to seller and issued a promissory note of approximately $82.6 million over 4.5 years.
- Company has the option to acquire all mineral titles contiguous with the land and will do so upon completion of scheduled payments.
- The acquisition allows Ivanhoe Electric to finalize the project's initial economic assessment, expected to be released in Q3 2023.
- Company combines advanced mineral exploration, renewable energy storage, and electric metals projects to reinvent mining for the electrification of everything, primarily in the United States.
